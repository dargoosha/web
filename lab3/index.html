News

The World Culture Festival 2024 in Karachi has become a significant highlight in the global arts scene.

The event, held at the Arts Council of Pakistan, showcases talents from over 40 countries, with a variety of cultural performances, including theatre, music, and dance.

One of the standout performances was White Rabbit, Red Rabbit, an internationally acclaimed play by Iranian playwright Nassim Soleimanpour. The play, performed without rehearsals, captivated audiences with its unique and experimental approach.

The festival, featuring more than 450 artists, underscores Pakistan's growing role in the international cultural landscape

About artist

The Weeknd, born Abel Tesfaye, is a Canadian singer known for his distinctive voice and dark, moody music style. He gained global fame with hits like "Blinding Lights" and "Starboy," blending R&B, pop, and electronic influences. Over the years, The Weeknd has won numerous awards, including multiple Grammys, for his unique contributions to modern music.
